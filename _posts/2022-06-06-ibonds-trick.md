Most people believe there is an absolute $10,000 yearly limit for buying electronic Series I Savings Bonds. You can buy some more using your personal tax return, but there’s a little known way to legally buy even more!

# Using Trusts

Remember, In the United States, trusts are technically their own legal entity. As such, a trust named “John Smith” would be distinct from an individual named “John Smith”. That’s pretty strange! 

So how does this help us? Well for example, if one had enough free time and money to set up five trusts with themselves as the trustee as follows:

- John Smith Trust 1
- John Smith Trust 2
- John Smith Trust 3
- John Smith Trust 4
- John Smith Trust 5

They’d now be able to buy upto $50,000 in Series I Savings Bond using five separate TreasuryDirect entity accounts.

Of course, this requires enough patience and willpower to use the TreasuryDirect website. (Buying and selling coming soon to TreasuryViewer 🤠)
